Our Story

More Than a Salon —
A Sanctuary

Gravity Saloon was founded in 2014 with a single dream: to give every woman a space where she feels truly celebrated. Over a decade, we've grown into a beloved beauty destination trusted by thousands of clients across the city.

Our team of expert beauticians combines traditional techniques with modern innovation to deliver results that speak for themselves. From everyday glam to once-in-a-lifetime bridal looks, we pour our heart into every appointment.

  • Certified Professionals

    Our beauticians are trained and certified with years of hands-on experience.

  • Premium Products Only

    We use only top-grade, skin-safe products from trusted brands.

  • Personalized Approach

    Every treatment is tailored to your unique skin type, hair texture and needs.

  • Hygienic & Safe

    Strict hygiene protocols and sanitised equipment for every client, every time.
